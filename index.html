What is shared hosting ?

In simple terms a web host or web hosting service provider, is a business that provides the technologies and services needed so that a website, a blog or webpage can be viewed on the Internet by people looking for your services. Website files are stored on special computers called servers which is one the services your hosting provider provides you with, space to store your web files. For example, If you created your own website using your own editing software then you should choose shared or vps hosting depending on your preference of sharing space with others or having your own private space or if you have a wordpress website then choose one of the four plans available to you.

What is a domain name ?

Domain names are essential for hosting your website on the internet. An example domain name is www.yoursitename.com. It's advisable to choose a name that isn't too long possibly making it difficult for people to remember. You should choose a name that best suits what type of business you run. Domain names are relatively inexpensive. once you've chosen a name, the next step is check it's availability and extension www.yoursite.com then register it with a (TLD) top level domain .com .eu .fr .us .au for around $8.95 for the first year.
